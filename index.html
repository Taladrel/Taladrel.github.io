Project Idea

The basic principle of my project idea is to form a phone application and website, which works in unison with each other. The website and app will connect farmers with potential seasonal workers and overseas working holiday visa holders, that need to complete their hours for their second and third year working visas. This app and website is designed to create a safe and simple platform for workers and farmers to be able to work together.

The motivation for creating this service comes from meeting my current partner Charlotte. When we first met she was on a working holiday visa and only planning to stay for one year. When we looked at getting the hours she needed to gain for her second-year visa, we also started to realize how hard it was to find local work for her. Not only that but it can also be quite dangerous as exploitation and abuse are not unheard of when it comes to backpackers.

The features I would like to implement in the application are:
  • A profile system for farmers will include the name of the farm, location, a simple job description, if tools or any previous experience are required and if accommodation is available on site. There will also be a feature that can recommend the nearest accommodation available to the workplace. For workers, the profile will include their name, previous workplaces and the location of where they are looking for work. Each profile type will be able to contact each other, to show that they are interested.

  • A rating system will be implemented for all profiles so farmers and workers are able to rate each other. This allows both parties to quickly tell and easily tell if something is wrong. This would be shown in a star system quickly visible to the user on the front of the profile they are looking at. With further information about each review available after opening the profile. This feature will help workers know that they are going to a safe environment and that others have been paid properly. It will also help farmers know if the person they are going to potentially employ is a responsible and hard worker.
